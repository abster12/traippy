Features

Everything in one place

Every trip gets its own space. Every item has its place.

Day-by-day itinerary

Build your plan with places, activities, and times. Clear, visual, easy to adjust on the go.

Bookings at a glance

Flights, hotels, rentals, experiences — confirmation status in one view. No email digging.

Budget & split

Track spending against your budget. Split equal, custom, or by percentage — always know who owes what.

Smart splitting

Expense ledger with settlement tracking. See who's settled up — no awkward group chat moments.

Private vault

Passport, visa, insurance, emergency contacts — secure and shareable only when you choose.

Built for groups

Invite friends. Everyone sees the plan, adds bookings, splits expenses, stays in sync.

Works without internet

Access your itinerary, bookings, checklists, and budget off the grid. Traippy is offline-first by design — not a premium add-on.
